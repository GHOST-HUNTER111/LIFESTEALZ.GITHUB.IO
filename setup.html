🎮 How to Join the Lifestealz SMP

Welcome to the official Lifestealz SMP setup guide. Follow these steps or your Minecraft will explode. (Not really… probably.)

🧱 Step 1 — Install a Mod‑Capable Launcher

You need a launcher that can run mods. Pick one:

  • 🔥 Prism Launcher (recommended)
  • 🌙 Lunar Client (works if you add mods manually)
  • ⚡ Any launcher that supports Fabric/Forge mods

If you try to join without mods, Minecraft will scream at you. Loudly.

🎤 Step 2 — Install Simple Voice Chat

This is REQUIRED. No voice chat = no entry. We’re basically a Discord server but inside Minecraft.

  • Download the Simple Voice Chat mod for your Minecraft version
  • Put it in your mods folder
  • Restart your launcher

If you join without it, you’ll sound like a mime. Don’t be a mime.

🧨 Step 3 — Join the Server

Once the event day arrives, open Minecraft and join:

SERVER_URL

Replace SERVER_URL with your actual IP or domain. If you join early, you’ll just stare at a wall. A very judgmental wall.

🎉 Step 4 — Have Fun (or Die Trying)

This is Lifesteal. You hit people → you gain hearts. People hit you → you lose hearts. Lose all hearts → skill issue.

Good luck. You’ll need it. 😈
